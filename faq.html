Frequently Asked Questions

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies depending on the size of your business and the specific services you need. I’m committed to providing affordable, personalized bookkeeping that fits your budget.


How do you charge for your services?

Most of my clients prefer a flat monthly fee, which provides predictability for both of us. Some of my clients, however, prefer that I charge hourly. We can work together to find a pricing structure that makes sense for your needs.


Do you only work with local businesses in Maine?

Nope! While I’m based in Maine, I work with clients all across the country!


What bookkeeping software do you use?

I primarily work with QuickBooks Online and have experience with Paychex as well.


Can you help me prepare for tax season?

Yes! I provide organized financial reports and records to help make tax preparation as smooth as possible for you or your tax professional.


What kinds of businesses do you work with?

I work with a variety of small businesses. Whether you’re just starting out or you’ve been in business for years, I’m here to help.


Do you offer one-time help or ongoing support?

Both! Whether you need a one-time cleanup or regular monthly support, we’ll build a plan that fits your business.


How do we get started?

Just reach out! We’ll start with a free consultation to discuss your needs and see if we’re a good fit to work together.
