About

Hi! I’m so glad you’re here. My name is Charlie and I’m a bookkeeper who’s passionate about helping small businesses thrive. My wife and I recently moved to Maine, and we’ve been loving building our life here together. Even though I’m based in Maine, I work with clients all over the country, from Florida to Indiana and everywhere in between. I help small business owners take the stress out of their finances so they can stop worrying about spreadsheets, receipts, or tax deadlines and focus on what they love. I started By the Book Bookkeeping because I genuinely love learning about how each small business operates. Every business has a unique story, and I’d be honored to be a part of helping those stories grow and thrive. I’m a QuickBooks Certified ProAdvisor with years of experience in both QuickBooks and Paychex, and I’m all about honesty, reliability, and making this process as simple and helpful as possible for you. Let’s build something great together.
